What is SeoLab®_

SeoLab® is a line of software products that range from managing industrial-size systems to simulating conditions, processes & results of quite a number of, well, systems. We are system professionals, our products are systems made for systems. Any person dealing with a long chain of products or customers knows how it can sometimes be very complex to monitor every little detail with minimal error. With a custom made SeoLab, the only thing you'd have to worry about is efficiency and innovation. SeoLab has very easy to understand graphic display that covers a wide range of variations all put in one place to make monitoring easier. You don't have to click buttons anymore to check individual status, do it with SeoLab.

Below is an example of an active SeoLab software running on the internal server of an Internet Service Provider. With our system, it becomes much easier to monitor subscriptions status, location and general performance of either an individual entity or an entire region. A threshold perfomance is set for every entity/region, when it's not met, our alert system goes as far as automatically reaching you through your phone no matter where you are in the world. You could monitor what you want, where you want, anytime, made possible by SeoLab.
